    About Us


    The Xavier’s Management Society (XMS) is a society formed under the Department of Business Administration and was established in September 2010. With the growing strength of students, the decision to form an association was an inevitable step towards progress. The Society is privileged to have the Vice Principal of the Business Administration and Commerce department, Father Dominic Savio S.J, as the President and Professor Baishali Agarwal as the Teacher-in-Charge to guide us in all its ventures. XMS is a society based on the ideological framework of commitment to excellence. With this very spirit, we in XMS believe it is essential for us to move out of our comfort zones and keep on questioning the need of moving ahead. Xavier's Management Society - is a new step taken by the Department of Business Administration, which seeks to make a collective effort in exploring avenues beyond the realms of academia. XMS is about bringing out the best in each student and providing an opportunity to display their potential. The Society aims at organizing different events throughout the year to maximize exposure and bring about an all-round development. All the students of the BBA department are automatically members of XMS. The Society seeks to develop new events and programs to enable students to explore their various skills and put their managerial knowledge to test. With activities like field trips, industrial visits, quizzes, panel discussions and debates, XMS provides a perfect channel to make a mark and leave an impression. XMS has also sent teams, representing the department, for participating in other management festivals. Our students shone out in each fest and returned with accolades and rewards alike.
